### Risk Management Strategies

Effective risk management is crucial for long-term trading success:

1. **Position Sizing**: Never risk more than 2-3% of your portfolio on a single trade
2. **Stop-Loss Orders**: Set predetermined exit points to limit losses
3. **Diversification**: Spread investments across multiple cryptocurrencies
4. **Dollar-Cost Averaging**: Regular purchases regardless of price fluctuations

### Market Analysis Techniques
